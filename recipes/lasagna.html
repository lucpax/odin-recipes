Lasagna

Description

This a recipe from the famous John Chandler, submitted years ago to the All recipes website.

Its a great option for the Sunday family lunch.

Ingredients

    Minced meat
    Onion and garlic
    Tomato sauce
    Sugar
    Lasagna noodles
    Cheese

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the riccota mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
